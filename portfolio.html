Easter Island

Easter Island is one of the most beautiful and secluded places on Earth. With it's vast landscape and towering Rapa Nui statues, this Polynesian island remains elusive and yet astounding in the modern age

Patagonia

Patagonia has been one of my most sought after destinations for a very long time. Not only is the trek extremely grueling but the views are more than worth the effort

Last updated 3 mins ago

Angkor Wat

Angkor Wat in Cambodia is the largest temple in the world, spanning across almost 200 acres. The artistry and architecture of the temple complex itself is most appealing to me, along with its rich history
